DEMAR DEROZAN 2017-2018 HIGHLIGHTS

About

Demar Darnell DeRozan was born on August 7, 1989 in Compton, California. He is currently an NBA player for the Toronto Raptors, and plays the position of the shooting guard. He is 6'7" tall (2.01 meters) and weights 220 pounds. DeRozan is averaging 23.7 PPG, and is shooting a career high 33% from 3 point land.

Achievements/Milestones

DeRozan has played for the Raptors for his entire career so far, and he and the team have had many respectable playoff runs, most notably the 2015-16 season, when they reached the conference finals, and lost to the Cleveland Cavaliers in 6 games.
